# Deep Customer Avatar Intelligence Worksheet

> Detailed worksheet framework for the avatar-builder skill.

Deep Customer Avatar Intelligence Worksheet
Purpose
The goal of this worksheet is to understand the customer so deeply that we can write copy, build offers, create content, craft hooks, handle objections, and design funnels that feel like they were made specifically for them.
This worksheet should be completed using:
* Customer interviews
* Sales call recordings
* Support tickets
* Reviews
* Comments
* DMs
* Survey responses
* Testimonials
* Failed sales conversations
* Competitor reviews
* AI transcript analysis
All ideal customer avatar interviews should be recorded, transcribed, and run through the AI profiling system.

Section 2: Surface Problem vs. Real Problem
What does the customer think their problem is?
This is the problem they would say out loud.
Examples:
* “I need more leads.”
* “I need to lose weight.”
* “I need better soil.”
* “I need to buy houses with no money down.”
* “I need more bookings.”
* “I need better content.”
Answer:
What is the real problem?
This is the deeper issue they may not fully understand yet.
Examples:
* They do not have a believable offer.
* They are attracting the wrong buyer.
* Their body is inflamed and stressed, not just overweight.
* Their soil biology is dead, not just low in NPK.
* They lack a repeatable acquisition system.
* They have no authority, trust, or proof.
Answer:
What is causing the real problem?
Answer:
What have they misdiagnosed?
Answer:
What are they blaming that may not be the real cause?
Answer:
What is the hidden mechanism behind their problem?
Answer:
What would they need to understand before they would agree with our diagnosis?

Section 3: Awareness Level
